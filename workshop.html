Our Crafting Space

Need a space for your knitting circle, drawing class or reading group?

Our crafting room is available to hire for all sorts of crafting and arts groups both within and outside of our opening hours.

Tea and coffee can also be provided.

To enquire or arrange to come and see the room get in touch by phone or by email. Alternatively pop in during opening hours and have a chat!

We also host a range of classes in our crafting room. Limited number of places so book early to avoid disappointment.
