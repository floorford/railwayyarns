EYB

Yarns specifically sourced from trade shows around Europe ideal for babies and children's wear.

Fyberspates

A company who make luxury yarns for hand knitting & crochet. Silk, Bluefaced Leicester, Alpaca and merino. Hand dyed and solid dyed.

Jamiesons of Shetland

Jamieson’s is a family owned business, which has specialised in wool from the native Shetland sheep for 5 generations. Their Shetland Tweeds in particular are fantastic.

John Arbon

Traditional British-made yarns designed, blended and spun using vintage machinery at their mill in the heart of North Devon.

Juniper Moon Farm

Shortlisted for the 2020 British Knitting & Crochet Awards, this company was founded in 2007 in Virginia and sources yarn from North and South America, Nepal and Italy.

Louisa Harding

Luxury yarns

Novita

Based in Finland. We stock their 7 Veljestä Aran range, Isoveli, Venla, Nalle, Veljestä Polaris, Veljestä Kuura and Veljestä Pohjola ranges.

Schoppelwolle

For more than 70 years, their traditional company has been synonymous with hand-knitting yarns “Made in Germany.”. The production takes place partly on self-developed and patented machines.

Sirdar

Sirdar are based in Yorkshire, UK and have been one of the leaders in the knitting yarn industry since 1880

West Yorkshire Spinners

We have a wide variety of WYS yarns including: BoPeep, Exquisite, Illustrious and Re:Treat

Wooly Knit

Based in Oldham, UK they specialise in 100% British Wool products but have lots of different types of wool!

King Cole

For over 80 years, King Cole has built a reputation for supplying the highest quality knitting wool and yarn to professional and hobbyist knitters worldwide.
